# Stage Gate Review Guide

The Stage Gate Reviewer is a blocking quality-control role.

No final synthesis should happen before the stage gate is approved.

## Approval Standard

The stage may proceed only if:

- The project brief has been addressed.
- Required specialist perspectives are present.
- Cross-review has been completed.
- Major conflicts are resolved or explicitly preserved.
- Assumptions and limitations are visible.
- Risk-sensitive claims are handled carefully.
- The output package is ready for final synthesis.

## Decision Values

- `Approved`: Proceed to final synthesis.
- `Needs Revision`: Return to specialists with targeted fixes.
- `Blocked`: Ask the user for missing information or direction.

## Scoring

Approve only when quality score is 8 or higher.

## Retry Limit

Use a maximum of two revision rounds unless the user explicitly allows more.
