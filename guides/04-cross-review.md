# Cross-Review Guide

Cross-review turns isolated specialist outputs into a stronger analytical package.

## Goals

- Detect contradictions
- Improve weak evidence
- Align assumptions
- Surface dependencies
- Reduce duplication
- Prepare for stage-gate review

## Process

1. Read every specialist output.
2. List agreements.
3. List conflicts.
4. List missing information.
5. Assign targeted revisions.
6. Require each specialist to revise its own output.
7. Produce cross-review notes.

## What Counts As A Conflict

- Different assumptions about customer segment
- Different views of feasibility or risk
- Contradictory market or competitor claims
- Product recommendations that conflict with technical constraints
- Go-to-market plans that conflict with pricing assumptions

## Output

Use `templates/cross-review-notes.md`.
