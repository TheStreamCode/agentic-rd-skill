# Quality Standards

Use this checklist to evaluate every output.

## General Quality

- Clear objective
- Direct relevance to the brief
- Specific findings
- Explicit assumptions
- Visible uncertainty
- Practical recommendations
- No unsupported certainty

## Evidence Quality

- Sources are cited when available.
- Claims are marked as evidence, inference, or assumption.
- Important unknowns are not hidden.
- Quantitative claims include context.

## Strategy Quality

- Recommendations are actionable.
- Trade-offs are visible.
- Risks are connected to mitigation options.
- Next steps are sequenced.

## Final Output Quality

- The output is coherent as one document.
- The output does not read like pasted agent notes.
- Conflicts are resolved or explained.
- The final recommendation follows from the analysis.
