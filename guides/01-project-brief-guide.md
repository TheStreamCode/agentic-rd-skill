# Project Brief Guide

The project brief is the source of truth for the workflow.

Use `templates/project-brief.md` and keep the brief specific enough that an agent can make useful decisions without inventing the project context.

## Required Fields

- Subject
- Goal
- Desired output
- Audience
- Constraints
- Scope boundaries
- Success criteria

## Good Subject Example

```text
Research a new SaaS product for AI-powered legal assistants focused on contract review for small and medium-sized businesses in the US and EU.
```

## Weak Subject Example

```text
AI legal SaaS.
```

The weak version does not define customer, geography, output type, or strategic focus.

## Brief Quality Checklist

- The agent can identify the target audience.
- The desired output is explicit.
- Constraints are visible.
- The project has boundaries.
- The user has stated what a useful answer looks like.
