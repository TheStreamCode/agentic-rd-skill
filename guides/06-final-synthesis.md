# Final Synthesis Guide

Final synthesis is the process of turning approved specialist work into a coherent deliverable.

## The Synthesizer Must

- Preserve the strongest findings.
- Remove duplication.
- Resolve or explain conflicts.
- Prioritize recommendations.
- Keep uncertainty visible.
- Align the output with the project brief.
- Include next steps.

## The Synthesizer Must Not

- Invent new major claims.
- Ignore specialist disagreements.
- Treat assumptions as facts.
- Convert informational analysis into professional advice.
- Add filler to make the report look longer.

## Recommended Final Output Structure

- Executive summary
- Context
- Findings
- Analysis
- Recommendations
- Risks and limitations
- Next steps
- Appendix
