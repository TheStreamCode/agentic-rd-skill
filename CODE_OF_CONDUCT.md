# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this project professional, constructive, and welcoming for people who want to improve agentic research and development workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas, prompts, and documentation without personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the library.
- Be careful when discussing regulated domains such as legal, medical, financial, security, and compliance use cases.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private, confidential, or sensitive information.
- Encouraging unsafe use of AI-generated outputs.
- Presenting generated outputs as qualified professional advice.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues, discussions, or contributions that do not follow this code of conduct.
