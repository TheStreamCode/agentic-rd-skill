# Example Project Brief: AI Legal Assistant SaaS

## Subject

Research a new SaaS product for AI-powered legal assistants focused on contract review for small and medium-sized businesses in the US and EU.

## Goal

Produce a research and product strategy report that evaluates market opportunity, customer needs, competitive positioning, MVP scope, technical feasibility, business model, risks, and go-to-market strategy.

## Desired Output

A professional product strategy report with practical recommendations and an MVP proposal.

## Audience

Startup founder, product lead, and technical co-founder.

## Context

The product idea is a SaaS platform that helps non-lawyer business users upload contracts, identify risks, summarize key clauses, compare terms against company playbooks, and prepare questions for legal counsel.

## Constraints

- Geography: US and EU
- Market: SMBs and startups
- Domain: Legal-tech, contract review, AI assistants
- Safety: Must not present outputs as legal advice
- Product: MVP should be realistic for a small founding team

## Scope Boundaries

Include:

- Market and competitor analysis
- Product positioning
- MVP feature set
- Technical architecture considerations
- Risk and compliance concerns
- Business model options
- Go-to-market recommendations

Exclude:

- Definitive legal advice
- Jurisdiction-specific compliance certification
- Production-ready implementation code

## Success Criteria

- Clear recommendation on whether the opportunity is worth exploring
- Specific MVP scope
- Clear risk boundaries
- Practical go-to-market strategy
- Honest assessment of differentiation challenges

## Preferred Language And Tone

English, professional, concise, evidence-aware, founder-friendly.

## Human Review Requirements

Any legal or compliance claims must be reviewed by qualified legal professionals before use.
